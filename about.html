About Us

We Are Leader In Water Purification Market

At Gayatri Water Purifiers, we are committed to providing safe, pure, and healthy drinking water solutions for every home and business. With 15 years of experience in the water purification industry, we specialize in Water Purification, Domestic & Commercial RO Systems, Spare Parts & Accessories, Service & Maintenance, and Home & Kitchen Appliances such as chimneys from Hindware, Livpure, Glen, and Elica. We provide sales and service for leading RO brands including Aquaguard, Eureka Forbes Ltd., Kent RO Systems Ltd., Livpure RO, Aceafresh RO, Pureit RO, Gen Series RO, Leepure RO, Blueshell RO, Pieross RO, Pureone RO, Local RO Companies, Commercial RO, and Industrial RO, catering to the diverse needs of our customers. Our dedicated service locations cover Bhadrak, Odisha, and New Dhamnagar, Bhadrak, ensuring reliable and timely support for all your water purification and appliance requirements.
